ABOUT THE GAME

SCIENCE-FICTION MYSTERY 3D FIRST-PERSON PUZZLE-SOLVING

In the silent orbit of a dead planet, a forgotten research station stirs back to life. An unknown signal has reactivated its systems, awakening the lights, the doors — and perhaps something else within.

The player, responding to this mysterious signal, must navigate the desolate halls, uncover the truth behind the station's fall, and survive its many hidden dangers.

Lost Orbit focuses on exploring an interconnected station and bringing its systems back online. Fight enemies, access terminals, and unlock new areas as you move forward. Progress comes from understanding how the station works and using it to your advantage, while dealing with the risks hidden inside.
