ABOUT THE GAME

SCIENCE-FICTION MYSTERY 2D PERSPECTIVE PUZZLE-SOLVING

In the silent orbit of a dead planet, a forgotten research station stirs back to life. An unknown signal has reactivated its systems, awakening the lights, the doors — and perhaps something else within.

The player, responding to this mysterious signal, must navigate the desolate halls, uncover the truth behind the station's fall, and survive its many hidden dangers.

Lost Orbit blends environmental storytelling with puzzle-driven progression. Explore interconnected rooms, reroute power, decode terminals, and unlock sealed sectors while managing limited time windows.

Every corridor, log, and machine is a clue. The station itself is the puzzle — an evolving labyrinth that opens as you master its systems.
